DUBLIN, IRELAND

Joey Weidner

Producer / Educator
"These are truly unique looking and sounding guitars. A blues machine and an absolute beast."

Biography Joey began his career as a session guitarist and songwriter on the Dublin music scene in 2013. His unique blend of Blues, Soul, and Rock has led him to work internationally with a diverse range of artists. His credits include work as a recording engineer for Kodaline, John Carney, Gary Clark, The Dropkick Murphys, and Linley Hamilton.

He regularly collaborates with brands such as D’Angelico Guitars, Milkman Sound, Victory Amps, Supro, Rotosound Strings, and Benson Amps.

Joey represents the versatility of Giungi Guitars, utilizing the instruments to bridge the gap between traditional Irish music and swamp/delta blues.

USA

Will Brennan

Robot Monster
"My Giungi is a clean machine for working out dirty riffs or shredding on classic licks."

Biography Will Brennan is the frontman of Robot Monster. A blues-rock guitarist from Connecticut with indie and punk influences, Will emerged on social media alongside drummer Logan Sidle, earning recognition from industry heavyweights including Skrillex, Fred Durst (Limp Bizkit), and Stone Temple Pilots.

His musicianship quickly drew the attention of prominent producers, allowing him to launch Robot Monster without sacrificing his stripped-down, groove-oriented sound.

Will relies on Giungi Guitars to deliver his signature tone—a blend of riff-heavy rock n' roll and hook-driven melodies.
