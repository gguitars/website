OUR STORY

Welcome.

I am Federico Giungi. I founded Giungi Guitars in 2017, starting in a small corner of my apartment, surrounded by sketches, tools, and sawdust.

Humble Beginnings "My early workshop was far from professional. I was carving wood in the living room, often struggling to find space for my car keys among the projects and wood shavings."

Shortly after, I moved into a dedicated workshop. This space allowed me to refine my craft, experiment with new designs, and build without limitations.

Giungi Guitars creates fully handmade instruments in Ancona, Italy. We blend innovation with traditional craftsmanship to produce custom guitars and basses designed to be timeless artifacts.
