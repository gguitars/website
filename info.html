Shipping

Giungi Guitars ships worldwide, except to the following countries: Algeria, North Korea, Belarus, Ukraine and Russia.

Shipping details here described apply to already existing products, such as those belonging to the category “Ready to play”.

Shipments are made through the following couriers: UPS, DHL, FEDEX, TNT.

The shipping of the instrument takes place within the next 24 hours following your purchase and the estimated time for you to get it depends on the national/international couriers and is detailed below, together with the prices.

FAQs

ORDER TRACKING

Shipments are traceable: when the order is handed over to the courier, the customer receives an automatic e-mail with the tracking code, through which they can follow the progress of the shipment, 24H on 24H, on the courier's website.

ORDER DELIVERY INFORMATION

We cannot ship an order to a country other than the country indicated in the billing address. We cannot deliver to PO boxes, personal mailboxes, APO (Army Post Office) or FPO (Fleet Post Office) military bases. In order to guarantee the service, it is your responsibility to actually pick up the order. We suggest that you arrange this in good time and contact us if you are unable to collect your order.
