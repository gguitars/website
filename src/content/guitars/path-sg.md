History_en: >
  The SG design features a thin and light body that contributes to a more
  comfortable playing experience, enhancing playability and making it a preferred
  choice for guitarists who enjoy soloing and exploring the upper register of the
  fretboard—this time without the famous neck-diving problem.
  The SG has a rich heritage, introduced in the early 1960s as the Les Paul model.
  Its association with iconic rock musicians, including Angus Young of AC/DC and
  Tony Iommi of Black Sabbath, has solidified its place in the rock and metal canon.
History_it: >
