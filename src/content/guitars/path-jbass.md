History_en: >
  The Jazz Bass dual-pickup configuration and independent volume controls
  offer a high level of tonal control. Players can blend the output from the bridge
  and neck pickups, providing a wide spectrum of tonal possibilities—from bright and
  crisp to warm and mellow.
  Its ergonomic design contributes to better balance and comfort while playing, allowing
  for extended sessions without shoulder pain.
  The Jazz Bass has a rich cultural and musical legacy. It became a favourite among
  pioneering bassists, including Jaco Pastorius, and its role in jazz, fusion, and
  many other genres has cemented its iconic status.
