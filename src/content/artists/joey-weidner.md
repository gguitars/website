Biography_en: 'Joey began his career as a session guitarist and songwriter on the
  Dublin music scene in 2013: Joey’s unique blend of Blues, Soul and Rock has led
  him to work all over the world with a diverse range of artists and producers, from
  Germany, to the US, and to participate as a Recording Engineer for Kodaline, John
  Carney, Gary Clark, The Dropkick Murphys and Linley Hamilton, among others.


  He regularly collaborates with brands such as D’Angelico Guitars, Milkman Sound,
  Victory Amps, Supro, Rotosound Strings, Benson Amps, Laney Amplification, Pickup
  Music and Giungi Guitars.


  Joey is one of the main artists for his capability to mix traditional Irish music
  and swamp/delta blues, embodying Giungi Guitars’ core values: uniqueness, bold and
  nonconformist style, and outstanding performances.'
