Initialization I am Federico Giungi and I founded Giungi Guitars in 2017, on the second floor of my flat, invading traditional furnishings with wooden models, tools and a lot, a lot of dust.

Historical Archive: The "Flat" Era "My sister wasn’t happy to host an 'almost real' artisan workshop at home, sometimes just laying the keys of the car would mean finding a space among projects, models and wooden curls (she was also sneezing a lot)."

Shortly after, I moved into a proper workshop, where I could eventually build and dirt, try, innovate, without boundaries.

Giungi Guitars is the brand of guitars and basses, fully handmade in Ancona, Italy, 100% customizable and that translates innovation, anti-conformity and superior quality into one single sample product, crafted to be a timeless artefact, designed to provide superior performances and satisfy the bravest creative minds.

My Main Drive & Values

Operating Parameters // With Guitars in Mind

Uniqueness

Giungi Guitars is unique. Giungi Guitars is a single sample product. Giungi Guitars is innovative and combines style, design and performance. You won’t find anywhere, anytime a guitar that could compete or even be compared to this one - no comparisons exist.

Superior Performance

Started to bring superior performance in the swamp/delta blues world, to fulfil the needs of brilliant minds and innovative artists, offering a product made to last, made to impress, made to make people watch and listen and feel.

100% Customizable

Any particular need? Appearance, neck or fingerboard measures? Customization increases the uniqueness of the guitar: a deeper connection is achieved by creating value together.

Non-Conformist

Born to fill the space between a unique product and a new and bold music expression. Giungi Guitars gets where standardised products and replicas can’t, allowing you to stand out from the crowd. Giungi Guitars is for the nonconformist artist that is in you.
